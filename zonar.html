What Zonar Does

Zonar provides GPS tracking, electronic verified inspections (EVIR), and fleet diagnostics for bus companies. It captures vehicle location, driver activity, and inspection data in real time. Zonar is a strong data platform — but the data it collects does not automatically turn into the proactive alerts your operations team needs to prevent service failures.

What Zonar Is Missing

No Driver No-Show Alerts

Zonar records sign-on activity but does not send you an SMS or email when a driver fails to show up for their scheduled route.

No Real-Time Speed Notifications

Speed data is logged but your team does not get an immediate alert when a bus exceeds your defined speed threshold.

No Proactive DVIR Alerts

Zonar captures inspection data but does not automatically notify you when a pre-trip or post-trip DVIR is missed or incomplete.

What Data BusFleetAI Reads From the Zonar API

Zonar provides an open API called the OMI (Open Mobile Interface) that gives third-party systems access to the full telematics dataset collected by Zonar's V4 hardware. BusFleetAI uses this API to monitor the data Zonar is already collecting from your fleet and convert it into automated alerts and reports your operations team can act on.

Additional Automation Services Available With Zonar

BusFleetAI's direct access to the Zonar OMI API enables the following automation services beyond core driver monitoring:

Driver No-Show Alerts (5-Minute Window)

BusFleetAI monitors Zonar sign-on events and compares them against scheduled start times. If a driver has not checked in within your defined window, dispatch receives an SMS and email alert immediately.

Real-Time Speed Violation Alerts

Zonar's OMI API returns continuous speed readings per vehicle. BusFleetAI evaluates every reading and sends an instant alert when any bus exceeds your defined threshold — driver, location, and speed included.

EVIR (DVIR) Compliance Monitoring

Zonar's EVIR system records electronic vehicle inspections. BusFleetAI monitors inspection completion and alerts you when a pre-trip or post-trip inspection has not been filed within your required window.

Hours of Service Compliance Alerts

BusFleetAI reads Zonar HOS data and alerts operations when a driver is approaching or has exceeded their available hours before a violation occurs.

Excessive Idle Monitoring

Zonar captures idle duration and fuel consumption per vehicle. BusFleetAI flags buses idling beyond your defined threshold and includes idle data in the daily summary for driver coaching.

Route Deviation and Geofence Alerts

Using Zonar's zone event data, BusFleetAI detects when a vehicle departs from its assigned route or enters or exits a defined geographic area, and sends an immediate alert to dispatch.

All services configured to your thresholds and delivered to your recipients by SMS and email. Contact us to discuss which combination fits your fleet.
