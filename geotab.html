What Geotab Does

Geotab provides GPS tracking, engine diagnostics, driver behavior scoring, and compliance tools for commercial fleets. It collects rich telematics data from every vehicle in your fleet — location, speed, idling, trip history, and inspection records. Geotab is a powerful data platform, but the data it collects does not automatically turn into the proactive, real-time alerts your operations team needs to prevent service failures.

What Geotab Is Missing

No Driver No-Show Alerts

Geotab tracks driver activity but does not send you an SMS or email when a driver fails to show up for their scheduled bus route.

No Instant Speed Notifications

Speed events are logged in reports but your team does not get an immediate alert when a bus exceeds your defined speed threshold on the road.

No Proactive DVIR Alerts

Geotab stores inspection data but does not automatically notify you the moment a pre-trip or post-trip DVIR is missed or incomplete.

What Data BusFleetAI Reads From the MyGeotab API

Geotab provides one of the most comprehensive fleet data APIs in the industry — the MyGeotab SDK. It exposes every data point the GO device collects from the vehicle's engine and telematics system. BusFleetAI connects to the MyGeotab API and uses its data feed services to monitor your fleet continuously and deliver automated alerts and reports without anyone having to watch a screen.

Additional Automation Services Available With Geotab

Because the MyGeotab API exposes the most complete dataset of the three platforms BusFleetAI integrates with, the following services are available for Geotab users:

Driver No-Show Alerts (5-Minute Window)

BusFleetAI reads Geotab DriverChange events and compares them against scheduled start times. If a driver has not signed on within your defined window, an SMS and email alert goes to dispatch immediately.

Real-Time Speed Violation Alerts

BusFleetAI reads LogRecord and ExceptionEvent data from MyGeotab and sends an instant alert when any vehicle exceeds your defined speed threshold — driver name, vehicle ID, location, and exact speed included.

DVIR Compliance Monitoring

BusFleetAI reads Geotab DVIRLog data and alerts your team when a pre-trip or post-trip inspection has not been filed on schedule. Defect records are included in the daily summary report.

Engine Health and Predictive Maintenance Alerts

Geotab StatusData exposes engine fault codes, oil temperature, battery voltage, and more. BusFleetAI monitors these values and alerts your maintenance team when a reading indicates an issue before it becomes a breakdown.

Hours of Service Compliance Monitoring

BusFleetAI reads DutyStatusAvailability data from MyGeotab and alerts operations when a driver is approaching or has exceeded their HOS limits before a violation is recorded.

Idle Time and Fuel Waste Reporting

BusFleetAI reads trip and exception data from MyGeotab to identify excessive idle time per vehicle and driver. Idle events are flagged in real time and compiled in the daily summary for targeted coaching.

All services are configured to your fleet's specific rules and delivered to the recipients you define by SMS and email. Contact us to discuss which combination fits your Geotab operation.
