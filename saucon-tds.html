What Saucon TDS Does

Saucon TDS provides bus fleet management, dispatch, scheduling, and vehicle tracking. It is the operational backbone for many charter and private bus companies, handling route planning, driver assignments, and real-time GPS tracking. It stores the data your fleet generates every day — sign-on times, vehicle locations, speeds, and inspection records.

What Saucon TDS Is Missing

No Driver No-Show Alerts

Saucon TDS tracks sign-on data but does not automatically alert you when a driver fails to show up for their scheduled route.

No Speed Violation Notifications

Speed data is recorded but nobody gets a real-time alert when a bus exceeds your defined threshold on the road.

No DVIR Compliance Alerts

Inspection data exists in the system but you have no automated way to know when a pre-trip or post-trip DVIR is missed.

What Data BusFleetAI Reads From the Saucon TDS API

Saucon provides a suite of API services for accessing hosted telematics data. BusFleetAI connects to four core services: the Asset Location Service for real-time GPS movement, the Asset History Service for 24-hour event logs per vehicle, the Engine Stats Service for engine lifetime values, and the Predicted Status Data Service for scheduled versus actual route arrival times. All four power BusFleetAI's automated alerts and daily summary reports.

Additional Automation Services Available With Saucon TDS

Because BusFleetAI has direct access to all four Saucon TDS API services, the following automation capabilities are available beyond the core driver no-show and speed monitoring package:

Driver No-Show Alerts (5-Minute Window)

BusFleetAI monitors the Asset History Service for driver sign-on events. If a driver has not signed on within your defined window after their scheduled start time, an SMS and email alert goes to your designated recipients immediately.

Real-Time Speed Violation Alerts

The Asset Location Service returns vehicle speed continuously. BusFleetAI evaluates every reading against your defined threshold and sends an instant alert when any vehicle exceeds it — driver name, vehicle ID, location, and speed included.

First Pickup Confirmation

BusFleetAI detects the first stop event on a route from the Asset History Service and sends a confirmation alert to dispatch — proof that service has begun without anyone having to call.

On-Time Performance Monitoring

Using the Predicted Status Data Service, BusFleetAI compares scheduled versus actual arrival times across active routes and flags deviations that exceed your defined threshold.

IFTA Mileage Reporting Automation

The Asset History Service returns state-by-state mileage per vehicle. BusFleetAI compiles this automatically and generates IFTA quarterly mileage reports, eliminating manual logging for fleets operating across state lines.

Daily Fleet Operations Summary

Every morning BusFleetAI delivers an automated summary covering overnight activity, no-shows, speed events, route completion status, engine hours, and total mileage across your fleet.

All services are configured to your fleet's specific rules and thresholds and delivered to the recipients you define. Contact us to discuss which combination fits your operation.
